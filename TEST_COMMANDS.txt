--- Water swim / shore rescue (2026-07-17, reworked) ---
Expect:
  - SWIM (forward+jump), not hop-in-place, not long bridges
  - Find land within ~36 blocks (dry or shallow)
  - Dig kelp/seagrass/soft block if stuck
  - Surface when O2 low; never false "Already on dry land"
  - stop frees emergency claim; follow must not say "path busy (emergency)"
  - Drowned-safe: keep moving toward shore, don't thrash

[ ] Owner in water → tj come here          — swim to you
[ ] Owner on far shore → tj come here      — swim across
[ ] Push tj into deep water → wait         — auto shore rescue
[ ] tj get to shore / water rescue
[ ] Mid-stuck: tj stop then tj come here   — claim free, moves again
[ ] After free: tj follow me across pond   — swim profile
